❓ QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING

  Problem: Time field doesn't appear when I toggle inhaler
  → Solution: Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R or Ctrl+Shift+R)

  Problem: Analytics tab shows "Not enough data"
  → Solution: Need 3+ logs with breathing & legs scores (give it a few days)

  Problem: Correlation chart looks empty
  → Solution: This is normal with <5 entries, builds up over time

  Problem: CSV export shows empty time columns
  → Solution: Correct! Empty means you didn't log that time that day
